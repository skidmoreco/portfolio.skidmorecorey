About me

Full Stack Software Developer

My Name is Corey Skidmore & I'm a Full Stack Software Developer. Software Development first peaked my interest in the spring of 2022. I wanted to learn more and didn't know where to start. With the help of a few friends, I decided to enroll in The Ohio State University's Full Stack Development Flex-program. I felt like jumping into the field at age 28 would be too late, but quickly realized, that is far from the truth. I have always appreciated a good challenge and have never been afraid of learning something new. I was ready to make this a career instead of a new-found hobby.
